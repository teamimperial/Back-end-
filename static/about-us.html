What is Praxis?

A simple platform for you.

There are companies who want to take students to practice. There is a situation where several companies are ready to take one student, but the student does not know about it, or the student wants to go to practice in a company that has already recruited a group. So, we have implemented a platform that will allow companies to declare the number of students they can practice and send out an invitation. Students will also be able to see which companies are interested in them, or in which companies have opportunities to study and apply.
